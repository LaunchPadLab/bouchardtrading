Careers

Job Title: Junior Trader

Job Duties
  • Trade various products on a number of different exchanges
  • Add programming knowledge to our current trading platforms and strategies
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree with emphasis on Finance or Economics
  • Software development experience in C++
  • Strong math skills with the ability to quickly compute problems
  • Compete in a high pressure environment
  • Willing to work night shifts or day shifts

Job Title: Sales Manager

Job Duties
  • Direct the sales initiative for a cutting edge automated algorithmic trading platform
  • Present demonstrations to potential clients and strategic partners
  • Travel to meet clients
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • Sales experience with trading software
  • Experience in the financial markets
  • Experience with Power Point and or other presentation software

Job Title: Sales Associate

Job Duties
  • Assist the sales manager in all aspects of our automated algorithmic trading platform
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • Strong presentation skills
  • Experience with Power Point and or other presentation software
  • Prior experience or knowledge in financial markets
