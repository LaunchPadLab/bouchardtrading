About Bouchard Trading

Justin R.R. Bouchard – Chairman/CEO

Mr. Bouchard started Bouchard Trading in the summer of 2010 after successfully cofounding DV Trading Chicago, a division of Rosenthal Collins Group in 2007.
Mr. Bouchard’s experience in the financial markets started in the summer of 2001, where he held an internship at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago. In the fall of 2002, he became a Series 7 Registered Representative with the National Association of Securities Dealers in the anticipation of his trading career beginning at TransMarket Group.
Justin is a proud graduate of the Boston University School of Management where he earned a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration with a Concentration in Finance. While earning his degree, Justin was a member of the Boston University men’s tennis team. He had become an avid tennis player as he grew up in Hinsdale, Illinois and attended Hinsdale Central High School.
Justin recently became happily married to his wife Julie.
