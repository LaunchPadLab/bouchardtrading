Justin Bouchard is the founder & CEO of Bouchard Trading. He is also the Founder & CEO of AlgoFast, a provider of high-speed, event-trading software for individual investors. A pioneer in the industry, he has experience trading in the fixed income, equity, energy, and currency markets. He started his career at Trans Market Group trading treasury cash and futures and quickly moved to the role of managing director at a trading firm that grew from three traders to 30 traders in three years. Today, Justin owns multiple seats on the CME and Bouchard Trading executes millions of contracts per year. He is a contributor on the topic of market structure in publications such as Wall Street and Technology and The Wall Street Letter. He is also a member of AT9000, the group responsible for pioneering quality control and industry standards for automated trading. He has been a speaker at the High Frequency Trading Show in Chicago, was invited to the UK Forex Awards as a nominee for "Best Algorithmic Trading Technology," and has been asked to speak at numerous international conferences on algorithmic trading in 2013. He is a graduate of the Boston University School of Management.
